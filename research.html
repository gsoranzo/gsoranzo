Research papers and presentations

  • Master thesis (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6634769): Thesis on the subject of reduction of Children drawings and trees. We gice here a criterion of good reduction for trees: a tree without have good reduction if it has good reduction at one critical point wisely ramified.
  • Article - Semi-stable model of Belyi children's drawings (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6427559): in this article we continue the work of M. Zapponi on semi-model of children's drawing by applying it on Belyi childrens drawing. You can see the effective construction of model here.
  • Presentation - Factorisation of Belyi like polynomial over finite fields (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6634838): we explain here a method of factorisation for Belyi like polynomials in finite fields, based on a lattice method.
