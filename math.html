Personnal mathematical notes and presentations

  • Master thesis (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6634769) (french): Thesis on the subject of reduction of Children drawings and trees. We gice here a criterion of good reduction for trees: a tree without have good reduction if it has good reduction at one critical point wisely ramified.
  • Presentation - Master thesis (french): Presentation of the Master thesis
  • Semi-stable model of Belyi children's drawings (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6427559): in this note we continue the work of M. Zapponi on semi-model of children's drawing by applying it on Belyi childrens drawing. You can see the effective construction of model here.
  • Factorisation of $X^n-\alpha$ over finite fields (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6789182): in this note we inspect the order of the irreducible factors of the polynomial $X^n-\alpha$ in finite fields $\mathbb{F}_p$.
  • Factorisation of $X^n-\alpha$ over finite fields - II (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6916423): In this note we explain factorisation of polynomials $X^n-\alpha$ with factor grouping irreducible polynomials of same order. We give a method to find this factorisation by induction.
  • Presentation - Factorisation of Belyi like polynomial over finite fields (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6634838): we explain here a method of factorisation for Belyi like polynomials in finite fields, based on a lattice method.
